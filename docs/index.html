❓ How to Play

🎰 Opening Cases

Select a case from the Cases tab. Each case costs coins to open. You'll roll for a skin of a random rarity. Blue is most common, Gold is the rarest. Higher rarity drops give more XP and are worth more coins.

💰 Earning Coins

Coins come from selling items in your inventory, claiming your weekly rewards, and fishing. The more you level up, the bigger your weekly payouts and fishing bonuses get.

🎁 Weekly Rewards

Check the Weekly tab each day for your daily reward. Most days give coins, mid-week gives a free case open, and Day 7 gives a premium case open. Miss a day and it's gone until next week.

🎣 Fishing

Head to the Fishing tab and hit Cast Line. You can catch coins, junk items worth coins, rare junk, or a real skin (2% chance) that goes straight into your inventory.

⭐ Favourites

Star any item in your inventory to protect it from Sell All. Favourited items are never sold unless you remove the star first.

⭐ Levelling Up

You earn XP every time you open a case or go fishing. The XP bar in the header fills up and when full you level up. Higher rarity drops give more XP. Check the Levels panel for a full breakdown of what each level unlocks.

🔵 Blue drop10 XP
🟣 Purple drop25 XP
🩷 Pink drop75 XP
🔴 Red drop200 XP
🟡 Gold drop500 XP
🎣 Fishing catch5–100 XP (scales with level)
🎁 Weekly reward150 XP
🔒 Case Unlocks

The first four cases are available from the start. Four more unlock as you level up: Spectrum at Level 5, Prisma at Level 15, Glove at Level 25, and the Classified Case at Level 40.

🏆 Leaderboard

Set your username on your first visit. Your stats sync automatically to a shared leaderboard. Enter the same username on another device to load your saved progress. Compete across three categories: Total Earned, Cases Opened, and Level.
