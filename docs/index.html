❓ How to Play

🎰 Opening Cases

Select a case from the Cases tab. Each case costs coins to open. You'll roll for a skin of a random rarity. Blue is most common, Gold is the rarest. Higher rarity drops give more XP and are worth more coins.

💰 Earning Coins

Coins come from three sources: selling items from your inventory, claiming your daily reward (resets at midnight), and fishing. The more you level up, the bigger your daily reward and fishing payouts get.

🎣 Fishing

Head to the Fishing tab and hit Cast Line. Something bites within a couple of seconds. You can catch coins, junk items worth coins, rare junk, or a real skin (2% chance) that goes straight into your inventory.

⭐ Levelling Up

You earn XP every time you open a case or go fishing. The XP bar in the header fills up and when full you level up. Higher rarity drops give more XP. Levelling up unlocks new cases, increases your daily reward, and boosts fishing payouts.

🔒 Case Unlocks

The first four cases are available from the start. Four more unlock as you level up: Spectrum at Level 5, Prisma at Level 15, Glove at Level 25, and the Classified Case at Level 40.

🏆 Leaderboard

Set your username on your first visit. Your stats are tracked automatically and pushed to a shared leaderboard your friends can see. Compete across four categories: Total Earned, Cases Opened, Best Item, and Level.
